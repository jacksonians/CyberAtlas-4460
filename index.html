    Key stats from recent research

    2,244 attacks per day
    Every 39 seconds
    30% increase in Q2 2024

    According to a study by the University of Maryland, a cyber attack occurs every 39 seconds, translating into an average of 2,244 attacks per day. These attacks are becoming increasingly common. In Q2 of 2024, global cyberattacks have increased by 30%.
